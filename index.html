Welcome

My name is Chris Hicks. This platform documents my fight to be an active, loving father in my daughter Nova's life — a fight against systemic obstacles and deeply troubling behaviours from those entrusted with her care.

Core Principle: Every child deserves safety, love, and access to both parents. Every parent deserves a fair process. This site exists to hold that line.

Our Story

Despite unwavering commitment to my daughter, I face daily barriers erected by Anouck Vigneau, her family, and their support network. Their actions have created a hostile environment that keeps Nova and me apart.

This site serves as a documented hub — evidence, updates, and calls to action — to shed light on these injustices and advocate for a fair, compassionate resolution. By sharing our experience, we work to protect Nova's rights and the rights of every child in similar circumstances.

Mission

This advocacy is evidence-driven and community-powered. The goals are clear:

  • Protect Nova — ensure her safety, wellbeing, and right to a relationship with her father.
  • Document — maintain a transparent, verifiable record of events and systemic failures.
  • Advocate — push for policy changes that prevent other families from enduring the same.
  • Connect — build a network of allies, professionals, and advocates who believe in justice.

Take Action

Our story speaks to broader issues of parental rights, systemic accountability, and child welfare. If you believe in justice:

  • Stay informed — check back for updates and new documentation.
  • Share this site with anyone who cares about children's wellbeing and parental fairness.
  • Reach out to policymakers, advocates, and organizations that can amplify our case.
  • If you have expertise in family law, child welfare, or advocacy — your voice matters here.
